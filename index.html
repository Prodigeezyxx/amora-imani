About Amora Imani

A modern atelier
in Knightsbridge, London.

We're Amora Imani, the go-to online destination for the confident, glamorous, fashion-savvy girl in search of that something extra special. Figure-flattering designs, body-contouring fabrications and quality are always at the heart of our brand.

Amora Imani is renowned for its statement designs, iconic silhouettes and exquisite embellishments that are made to be seen. The team prides itself on possessing a thorough understanding of composition, form and fit — they know that clothes serve a purpose and must be functional, but also that how a garment makes the wearer feel is pivotal.

Amora Imani is about statement designs and iconic silhouettes — reimagined for the modern woman who wants to stand out. We bring you fresh looks from independent designers alongside our own collections.

We also host a marketplace for independent designers to grow through our store — quality-checked, curated, and delivered worldwide. We love to collaborate with independent and small businesses to facilitate their growth through our platform.
